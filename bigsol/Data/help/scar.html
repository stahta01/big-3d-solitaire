Scarab

Object

To have eight sequences of cards going down from King down to Ace in the foundation.

How to play

Build down regardless of any suit.
Sequences of cards in any suit can be moved as a unit.
Empty piles can be filled with any card or legal sequence.

Clicking on the Stock pile at any time deals a card face up to every pile.
However, all piles must be non-empty.
A sequence of cards going down from King down to Ace can be moved to a foundation pile.
Once there, these cards are no longer in play.

Settings

Automatic in on by default and can't change it.
