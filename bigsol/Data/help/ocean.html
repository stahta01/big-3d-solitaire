Oceans' Eleven

Object

Game of three decks.
Move all of the cards to the foundations, building up in suit from ace to king.

How to play

Cards in the Tableau are built down by alternating color.
Groups of cards can be moved if there are enough Reserve piles free to allow the move if the cards were moved singly.
An empty pile in the Tableau can be filled with a King or group of cards start with a king. Foundations are built up in suit from Ace to King.
Cards in Foundations are not in play. Cards in Reserve piles can be played back on to Tableau or on to the Foundation.

Settings

Automatic in on by default
