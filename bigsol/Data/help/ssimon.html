Simple Simon

Object

To have four sequences of cards going down from King down to Ace in the foundation.

How to play

Build down regardless of any suit.
Sequences of cards in same suite can be moved as a unit.
Empty piles can be filled with any card or legal sequence (same suite).

A sequence of cards going down from King down to Ace can be
moved to a foundation pile.
Once there, these cards are no longer in play.

Settings

Automatic in on by default and can't change it.
