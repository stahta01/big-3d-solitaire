Towers

Object

Move all of the cards to the foundations, building up in suit from ace to king.

How to play

Cards in the Tableau are built down by the same suite.
Groups of cards can be moved if there are enough Reserve piles free
to allow the move
if the cards were moved singly.
An empty pile in the Tableau can be filled with a King or group of cards.
Foundations are built up in suit from Ace to King.
Cards in Foundations are not in play.
Cards in Reserve piles can be played back on to Tableau or on to the Foundation.

Settings

Automatic in on by default
