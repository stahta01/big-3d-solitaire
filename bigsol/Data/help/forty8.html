Forty and Eight

Object

Game of one decks
Move all cards to the Foundation piles.

How to play

Build down in suit
The top card of each pile is available for play to another tableau pile or to the foundations.
Only one card at a time can be moved.
Spaces may be filled with any available card.

One redeal is available.

Cards in Foundations are not in play.

Settings

Automatic in on by default.
