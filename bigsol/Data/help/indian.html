Indian

Object

Game of two decks
Move all cards to the Foundation piles.

How to play

Build down by any suit except the same.
The top card of each pile is available for play on another tableau pile or on the foundations. Only one card at a time can be moved.
Spaces may be filled with any available card.
The top card of waste is available for play on the foundations or the tableau.

There is no redeal.
Cards in Foundations are not in play.

Settings

Automatic in on by default.
