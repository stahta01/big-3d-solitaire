Russian

Object

Move all of the cards to the foundations, building up in suit from ace to king.

How to play

Russian Solitaire is similar to Yukon.

All face-up cards in the tableau can be moved onto another column by suite regardless of how deep they are in the pile.

The main objective is to clear the board of cards by organizing them, in ascending order, into four suits at the right of the game window, starting with the aces.

An empty pile in the Tableau can be filled with a King or a group of cards with a King on the bottom.

Settings

Automatic in on by default and can't change it.
