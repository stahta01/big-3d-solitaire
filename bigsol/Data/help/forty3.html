Forty Thieves Three Decks

Object

Game of three decks
Move all cards to the Foundation piles.

How to play

Forty eight cards in 12 columns of 4 cards each
Cards in the Tableau are built down by the same suite.
Only one card can be moved. Empty piles can be filled with any card.
The top card of each pile is available for play on another tableau pile or on the foundations.
Turn up one card at a time from the stock to the waste by clicking.
The top card of waste is available for play on the foundations or the tableau.

There is no redeal.

Cards in Foundations are not in play.

Settings

Automatic in on by default.
