Emperor

Object

Game of two decks.
Move all of the cards to the foundations, building up in suit from ace to king.

How to play

Emperor Solitaire is similar to Kondike, except there is no pile to draw from; all of the cards are dealt into the tableau, the ten columns in the middle. Cards in the Tableau are built down by alternating color.
Groups of cards can be moved.
An empty pile in the Tableau can be filled with any card or a group of cards.
Cards are flipped from the Stock to the Waste individually.
No redeals
Top card in Waste is in play.
Foundations are built up in suit from Ace to King.
Cards in Foundations are not in play.

Settings

Automatic in on by default.
