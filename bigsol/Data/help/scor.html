Scorpion

Object

Create four piles of thirteen cards each, each pile consisting of one suit and in rank order.

How to play

Cards in the Tableau are built down by suit.
Groups of cards can be moved regardless of sequence.
An empty pile in the Tableau can be filled with a King or a group of cards with a King on the bottom.
At any point, clicking on the Stock will deal the last three cards, one each on the first three piles.

Settings

Automatic in on by default and can't change it.
